Buldak

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • ramen
  • spicy sauce
  • chili oil
  • scallions
  • shallots
  • powder from packet

Steps

  1. first get your noodles and boil them
  2. then while its boiling add your spicy sauce
  3. while that's mixing you want to add your powder from the packet
  4. stir it up
  5. add your vegetables like scallions, shallots, bell pepper
  6. finnaly you want to top it off with some eggs and chili oil to add signifcant flavor
