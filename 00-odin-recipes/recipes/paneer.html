Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

basically its just cottage cheese with sauce and onions and other spices

  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • corriander
  • onions
  • heavy cream

Directions

  1. first make the paneer using cottage cheese
  2. then make the sauce by mixing the tomatoes and heavy cream
  3. add the paneer into the sauce in the pan and mix
  4. add your chopoped vegetables into this mix to get that perfect flavour
